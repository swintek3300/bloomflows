About Tony Flowers Inc

Our Story, Mission, and Values

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by master florist Tony Martinez, Tony Flowers Inc began as a small flower shop with a big dream: to bring joy and beauty into people's lives through the art of floral design. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a thriving business serving thousands of customers annually.

Our Mission

To provide the finest quality flowers and create stunning arrangements that exceed our customers' expectations. We believe every bouquet should tell a story and convey emotions that words cannot express.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the freshest flowers from trusted growers
  • Creativity: Each arrangement is a unique work of art
  • Service: Customer satisfaction is our top priority
  • Sustainability: We practice eco-friendly methods in all operations
  • Community: We proudly support local growers and charities

Meet Our Team

Our team of 15 skilled florists brings decades of combined experience. Led by Tony Martinez Jr., we continue the family tradition of excellence in floral design. Our designers are certified by the American Institute of Floral Designers (AIFD) and regularly attend workshops to stay current with the latest trends.

Detailed Service Information

Wedding & Event Services

Our wedding team provides comprehensive consultation services to bring your floral vision to life. Services include:

  • Initial consultation and venue visit
  • Custom design proposals with photos
  • Bridal bouquets and bridesmaid flowers
  • Ceremony arrangements and aisle decorations
  • Reception centerpieces and table arrangements
  • Setup and breakdown services
  • Rental items (vases, candelabras, arches)

Booking: We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings. Contact us to schedule your consultation.

Delivery Information

Local Delivery: $15 flat rate within 10 miles. Same-day delivery available for orders placed before 2 PM.

Extended Area: $25-$50 depending on distance (up to 50 miles).

Nationwide: We partner with FTD and Teleflora for nationwide delivery to ensure your flowers arrive fresh anywhere in the country.

Delivery Times: Standard delivery window is 9 AM - 6 PM. Specific time requests accommodated when possible for an additional $20 fee.
